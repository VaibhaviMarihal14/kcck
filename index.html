Patients who are suffering from any of the following problems/conditions are treated here with personal attention and best satisfaction

We provide treatment for the problems related to skeleton or orthopedic system. It mainly includes the treatment of disorders and injuries of orthopedic system. It also includes the treatment of back pain, degenerative and herniated disc disease, cervical spondylitis, frozen shoulder, neck pain, tennis elbow, meniscus tears and many more. We also provide treatment for pre and post-operative rehabilitation of shoulder, hip and knees. Treatment can relief your pain, improve your body strength, increase joint range and flexibility and brings back the patient to improved condition.

Some human neurological conditions directly affects the central nervous system due to which the patient feels difficult to walk, stand, move and sit. The neurological conditions such as a brain/head injury, stroke, motor neurone disease, multiple sclerosis, post polio syndrome, spasticity, parkinsonism, spinal cord injury and many more are treated by neurological physiotherapist. Our physiotherapists can provide a treatment for these conditions and provide you a good mental health.

Sports injuries are different from the regular injuries. But for an athlete fitness is very important. These injuries may happen due to overtraining, exercising and lack of conditioning. An inappropriate training/exercising technique can leads to a fracture, sprains, patellar tendonitis, twisted ankle, muscle cramp, shoulder stability and many more. We have specialised and expert physiotherapists to recover you from that pain and help you to maintain your fitness. With the rapid recovery we also provide an education for prevention of future injuries.

Cystic fibrosis (CF) is a disorder which directly affects the lungs due to which patient face difficult to breath. Chest infections, a cough and weight gain are the symptoms of CF. Secretions in lungs makes patient lazy. When we talk about babies, who are suffering from CF, will be having abnormally thick mucus. This blocks the airways and cause an infection in babies. Our physiotherapists provide the treatment to clear the airways (chest physiotherapy). We also provide medicine treatment for patients to clear lungs secretions by preventing lung collapse. Our physiotherapist provide antibiotics treatment so that the breathing efforts are reduced and area of lungs is improved.

In many cases children from the birth time only face some specific problems or issues like asthma, ear infection, allergies, kidney problems, sleep disorder, blood disorder, bone problems, growth problems, and many more. These problems has to be treated as soon as possible. Kleemcare provides many Diagnostic tests and procedures in order to prevent the children diseases and illness. We do focus on proper child growth and take care of their behavioural, mental and Physical health. We also provide the weekly treatment for the children till the age of 16.



How we are different

NO CLIENT OVERLAP

All appointments are spent with your physiotherapist focusing only on you. Our goal is to spend more one on one time with our clients providing thorough assessments, treatments, and education. No overlapping of clients during their sessions.

MOBILE ASSESSMENT/TREATMENT

For some individuals, leaving their place of residence may not be an option. Our physiotherapist will come to you if you live within Bangalore. Assessments and treatments can be completed in the privacy of your own home.

SOFT TISSUE RELEASE/JOINT MOBILIZATION

Active and passive muscle release and joint mobilizations can assist with increasing range of motion prior to exercise and can relieve pain and discomfort in the short term. Soft tissue release is beneficial to strengthen these increased ranges of motion for long term results.

HOME EXERCISE PROGRAMS

TAll clients will be provided with a home exercise program to empower them to take part in their rehabilitation and fitness goals. The goal is to never stop moving. Modifications and alternative exercises will be provided so the client can remain active while working through their recovery.

VIRTUAL PHYSIOTHERAPY

The use of web based video conferencing systems to allow you and your physiotherapist to connect via video on your computer or smart phone via web camera to receive physiotherapy services at a distance when an in-person visit is not possible.
